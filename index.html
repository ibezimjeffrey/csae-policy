Child Safety Standards Against CSAE

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

At S.T.E.P :Hire & Earn on Campus, we are committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe environment for all users.

Our Standards

  • We prohibit any child sexual abuse or exploitation content.
  • We prohibit grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors.
  • Violating accounts may be suspended or permanently banned.

Reporting

Users can report inappropriate content or accounts through the app or by contacting:
